About Madhouse

Custom prints with real character.

Madhouse Prints is all about bold 3D printed pieces that stand out the second you see them — from LEGO-inspired builds and articulated skeleton creatures to collectible figures with a lot more personality than the usual shelf filler.

Every piece is made to catch eyes, start conversations, and give collectors something fun, weird, and memorable to bring home. Whether it’s a fan favorite or a custom idea, the focus is on prints that look good and feel worth showing off.
