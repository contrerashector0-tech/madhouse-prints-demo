The Maker Behind the Madness

Hi, I'm the person running this whole operation out of El Paso — a full-time print nerd with a garage full of printers and a serious problem with buying new filament colors. What started as printing a few dinosaurs for my kids turned into a full-blown obsession with designing articulated figures, experimenting with exotic filaments, and finding ways to celebrate our border culture through the things I create.

Every piece that leaves my workshop is printed, checked, and packed with care. I love what I do, and I love sharing it with this community. Whether you're a collector, a gifter, or someone who just wants the coolest desk toy in the office — welcome to Madhouse Prints. It's a little chaotic here, and that's exactly the point.
